What each attachment family tends to do (practical)


‚Ä¢ Muzzle: recoil shaping, suppression/flash control (where available).
‚Ä¢ Barrel: effective range, bullet velocity, recoil behavior.
‚Ä¢ Underbarrel: recoil/aiming stability while moving or sustained fire.
‚Ä¢ Magazine: mag size & reload times; sometimes movement/ADS hits.
‚Ä¢ Rear Grip: handling‚ÄîADS speed, sprint/slide/dive-to-fire timings (great with Omnimovement).
‚Ä¢ Stock / Stock Pad: strafe speed, gun kick control, aim stability.
‚Ä¢ Laser: hip-fire spread, sprint-to-fire, ADS responsiveness (mode-dependent visibility).
‚Ä¢ Optic: sight picture preference; minimal stat effect beyond ADS speed.
‚Ä¢ Comb (snipers): scope behavior and handling.
‚Ä¢ Fire Mods: alter fire behavior (e.g., burst/auto conversions on certain guns). Example: SVD Full-Auto Mod (event reward) flips the SVD to full auto with rebalanced recoil/damage and blocks some other slots.
